Glow Essence Cosmetics | Siteplan

Site Name

This name was selected because it conveys radiance and elegance. “Glow” suggests healthy, luminous skin, and “Essence” implies purity and quality, aligning with a skincare-focused cosmetics brand.

Optional domain: glowessencebeauty.com

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the Glow Essence Cosmetics website is to showcase and sell affordable, cruelty-free beauty and skincare products. It will offer detailed product information, tutorials, and customer support to help users make informed choices and improve their self-care routines.

Scenerios:

  • Which foundation is best for sensitive or acne-prone skin?
  • How can I learn to apply makeup for a natural everyday look?

Color Scheme:

  • Soft Rose Pink (#F7C6C7): Used for buttons, headers, and accent elements to create a warm, inviting feel.
  • Gold (#D4AF37): Used for highlights, icons, and call-to-action hover effects to add a touch of luxury.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Background color for a clean, minimal look.
  • Charcoal (#333333): Used for body text to maintain readability.

Typography

  • Playfair Display (serif): Used for main headings and logo to give a stylish, premium feel.
  • Lato (sans-serif): Used for body text and navigation for clarity and modern readability.
